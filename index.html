My Story


My journey to becoming a web devoloper has not been a traditional journey. I have always wanted to make and impact and enjoy helping others. In grade school I was always involved a leadership role and helping make a change. Using my voice to help others was impactful and I decided that as a career I would get a degree in Political Science and see where this lead.

I spent almost 5 years in the Colorado General Assembly in different capacities as a Legislative Aide, Lobbyist and Campaign Intern. This then lead me to sell within large Fortune 500 companies including Thomson Rueters, ADP and DIRECTV Inc. I have found tremendous success in helping to generate over $200 million in business for these companies but lately have been ready for a new challenge.

Technology has always been an interest of mine and I decided to pursue a full stack development certification from the University of Denver. This will allow me to help companies and others in a new capacity. Check out some of my projects below and contact me through my form at the bottom of this page.
